Principles of Engineering Design
# Design Strategies for Ecological Engineering Clearly state the problem and begin to identify various physical, ecological, economic, legal, social, and ethical constraints. Research the problem and identify appropriate engineering techniques to analyze possible solutions. Take a look at other solutions to the problem (or similar challenges) and analyze why they work – or don’t work. Your goal is not to copy others’ work, but to try out different perspectives and gather information on a problem.
