# Design Strategies for Ecological Engineering Authors: John Bolte1 Gerrad Jones1, Desiree Tullos1, John Selker1 1Biological & Ecological Engineering Department, Oregon State University
[Design Process - Steps]    [Communication and Documentation] ## Fundamentals of Engineering Design Clearly state the problem and begin to identify various physical, ecological, economic, legal, social, and ethical constraints. Ecological engineering designs often incorporate additional constraints related to sustainability, system resilience, and need to provide a variety of ecosystem services, the geospatial nature of many ecological engineering problems, and the intrinsic complexity and variability associated with ecological systems.
